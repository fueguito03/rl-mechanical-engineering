# Why

This file answers one question.

Why am I studying Reinforcement Learning?

Whenever I lose motivation,
I should read this file.

Current answer

I am fascinated by how intelligence emerges through interaction with the environment.

I want to understand why humans learn efficiently,
why current reinforcement learning still struggles,
and whether entirely new learning paradigms can be developed.

Isaac Sim is not the goal.

Robotics is not the goal.

Algorithms are not the goal.

Understanding intelligence is the goal.
